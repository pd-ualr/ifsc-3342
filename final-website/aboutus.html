Our History

See our journey of making things more technological.

2005

Where it all started

Wired In is founded by John Smith in Little Rock. After a few years of hard work, John decided to curate his newspaper to focus more on tech, which proved to be fruitful as it became the leading newspaper in Little Rock.

2010

Hard work is all it is

With some support from the Locals, Wired In decided to extend it's branches towards more national technology related news, lauching their own radio program alongside it.

2015

Breaking new grounds

Expanding nationally proved to be useful for Wired In as they managed to snag a Pulitzer prize for investigative journalism, becoming a worldwide sensation.

2020

Trying to survive

During the pandemic, Wired In suffered majorly due to the COVID-19, and so, Wired In switched their narratives and focused on becoming an online prescence.

2023

Turning a new leaf

As the pandemic disappred, Wired In slowly heals; Bringing you fresh tech news. Glad for you to be here.
